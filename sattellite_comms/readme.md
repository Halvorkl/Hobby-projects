## Communications system: Lyra – GHOST mission

This project is the development of a 433 MHz CubeSat communications system.

I designed a PCB based on the TI CC1200 sub‑GHz transceiver, tailored for reliable low‑data‑rate satellite communications. In addition, we developed a custom antenna that was mechanically integrated into the spacecraft frame.

I cannot share the hardware design files, but the final project report is available in this folder.

The satellite launched in November 2025
