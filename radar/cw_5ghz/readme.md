## 5 GHz CW radar

This project is focused on learning radar fundamentals by designing a simple continuous-wave (CW) radar operating at 5 GHz.

The primary focus is on the analog front-end:
- RF generation and distribution
- TX/RX paths and front-end architecture
- Gain, noise, and linearity considerations in high-speed designs

The readout and digital parts of the system are intentionally kept very simple, serving mainly to validate the analog design rather than implement advanced processing.
